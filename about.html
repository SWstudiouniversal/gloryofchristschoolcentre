Glory of Christ Nursery and Primary School


The project started in 2015 as a Christian charity project with forty (40) children, in a grass thutch huts for prayers/church and classrooms (kindergarten one to three, (K 1-K3). Five volunteers, care takers sacrifice to serve, monitor, care and teach the children. In the same year we had nine (9) gradaunts (K.3) top class who perform well in reading and writing, numeracy, hygiene, English language and physical education (sport). The village health team voluntarily came in to check, treat, monitor and carry referral for those children in bad health condition. In 2016 we enrolled sixty children from nursery to primary one. The construction of permanent latrine was completed, one permanent block of class was raised with four classrooms up to gable level. Primary two class was introduced with eighty children in 2017. In 2018, school center open small compound, playground for physical education with local play materials. One hundred children were enrolled and primary three was introduced. In 2019, temporary grass thutch classrooms, dormitories were constructed with an increase in the enrollment of one hundred twenty four children. Primary four class was introduced. The school Centre open well in 2020 with enrolment of one hundred thirty five children. Primary Five was introduced. We are requesting faithful well hearted volunteer now to come stand and support school Centre build classrooms, dormitories, drill bore hole, sponsors children with books, food, tuitions (fees), medically and spiritually in Jesus Christ teach and pray for them.


Our Vision

To educate girls, orphans and children with special needs.


Our Mission

  1. To promote basic formal education.
  2. To promote psyhycho-social support for children with trauma.
  3. To provide basic needs for children example medical care, food, shelter and education.
  4. To train and raise children in good Christian (Pentecostal) morals.
  5. To promote equal right for all the children.


We hope to be the best in providing quality educational services in Uganda.

We are located in Uganda, Northern region, Gulu city, Western division, Oitino parish, Paminano ward, Lwalakwa village.
